# School Health

School Health is a Frappe/ERPNext app for school nurse offices. It helps nurses
record student health incidents, symptoms, care given, guardian communication,
follow-ups, and simple health trends.

The app includes:

- Student health incident tracking with severity, symptoms, care, and outcomes.
- A reusable health symptom library.
- Student health profiles for allergies, chronic conditions, medications, and care plans.
- Nurse-focused reports for incident analytics, repeat visitors, and symptom trends.
- A School Health workspace with number cards and dashboard charts.
- A migration patch that imports legacy `Health Incident`, `Heath Symptoms`, and
  `Symptoms Selection` records when they exist on a site.
